About

One of the first startups in Greece focused on post-quantum cryptography.

NBQ Engineering is an ambitious Greek-based tech startup dedicated to building the next generation of secure infrastructure. We help organizations prepare for the quantum era by supporting the migration of existing systems and the transition toward post-quantum secure technologies.

01 / Principle

Privacy First

We believe privacy must remain a fundamental right in the digital era. Every solution we build embeds confidentiality from the ground up and not added as an afterthought.

02 / Execution

Applied PQC Engineering

We combine advanced research knowledge with practical execution turning post-quantum cryptographic standards into deployable, real-world solutions.

03 / Future Readiness

Long-Term Resilience

We design with durability in mind, helping organizations build systems prepared for emerging threats including threats that have not yet fully materialized.

Why This Matters

The quantum threat is real and the clock is running.

Advances in quantum computing are expected to challenge widely used public-key cryptography. "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" attacks are happening today. Adversaries collecting encrypted data now, targeting it for decryption when quantum capabilities mature. Preparation cannot wait for Q-Day.
